Taj Mahal, Agra

    The Taj Mahal is an iconic white marble mausoleum located in the city of Agra, Uttar Pradesh
    Built in the 17th century by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan as a monument to his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world
    Taj Mahal is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world
    Its stunning architecture, intricate marble carvings, and symmetrical gardens make it a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of love and romance

Jaipur , Rajasthan

    Jaipur, also known as the "Pink City," is the capital of the Indian state of Rajasthan.
    Known for its majestic forts, palaces, and vibrant bazaars, Jaipur is a popular tourist destination and a hub of Rajasthani culture and heritage.
    Key attractions in Jaipur include the Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), and Jantar Mantar observatory.

Kerala Backwaters

    Kerala Backwaters are a network of interconnected canals, lakes, and lagoons located in the southern Indian state of Kerala
    Often referred to as the "Venice of the East," the backwaters offer a serene and picturesque environment surrounded by lush greenery and coconut palms.
    Visitors can explore the backwaters aboard traditional houseboats known as "kettuvallams," which offer a unique opportunity to experience Kerala's rural life, scenic beauty, and tranquil ambiance.
